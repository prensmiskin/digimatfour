About

Randa Company

Randa Electronics Ltd. Sti. was established in 1998 to design and produce products that provide convenience in everyday life. Considering the importance of being a standard and a brand (trademark) in products, in 2008, it registered the “Digimat” trademark. Production phases including designs, software, research and development, and products are done by Randa Electronik organization. It holds the honor of service qualification certificates from ISO 9001: 2008 Quality Management, ISO 27001: 2005 Information Management System documents, and TSE Service Qualification Certificate. As Randa Electronics Family our policy; Always keeps honesty in all our activities, raising products’ quality, provide sales and after sales services, and pay the highst level of attention to the customer needs. substantially our goal is the customer satisfaction.
